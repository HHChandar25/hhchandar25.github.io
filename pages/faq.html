FAQ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Upload your document to print.hz.nl, log in with your HZ account, and select the file. Then, go to any HZ printer, scan your student card, and release the print job.

How can you scan a document and send it to your PC at HZ?

Place your document in the HZ printer, log in with your student card, click "Scan", and select “Scan to email”. The file will be sent directly to your HZ email or stored in your personal folder.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

Visit the HZ Webshop, log in with your HZ account, select the product or service you need (such as software licenses, lab materials, or printing credits), and complete the payment online.

How can you book a project space?

You can reserve a project room via the Self-Service Portal or by using the HZ app. Log in, check available time slots, and confirm your booking.

What are the instructions at the HZ parking lot?

You can park in the designated student and staff parking areas. Use your HZ card to enter if required, and always follow the marked signs and traffic directions. Bicycles should be placed in the bike racks provided.
